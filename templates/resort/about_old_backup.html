OUR STORY

Banbas Resort was born from a simple dream: to create a luxury retreat that honors and celebrates the incredible natural heritage of Chitwan National Park. As locals who grew up surrounded by this wilderness, we understood that true luxury isn't about dominating nature—it's about becoming part of it.

Our journey began with a vision to build something different. Not just another resort, but a destination where guests could experience the raw beauty of Chitwan while enjoying world-class comfort and hospitality. Every decision, from the architecture to the landscaping, was made with deep respect for the land and its wildlife.

"We didn't just build a resort. We created a bridge between civilization and wilderness, where every guest becomes part of Chitwan's story."

Today, Banbas stands as proof that luxury and sustainability can coexist. Our resort employs local craftsmen, sources from local suppliers, and works closely with conservation efforts to protect the very ecosystem that makes our location so special. When you stay with us, you're not just a guest—you're part of our mission to preserve Chitwan for future generations.

What We Stand For

Our values guide everything we do, from how we treat our guests to how we care for the environment.

Sustainability First

Every aspect of our resort is designed with environmental responsibility in mind. From solar power to water conservation, we're committed to leaving a positive footprint.

Community Partnership

We believe in empowering local communities. Our team is 90% local, and we source materials and services from Chitwan businesses whenever possible.

Authentic Hospitality

Nepali hospitality isn't just a service—it's a way of life. We treat every guest like family, sharing our culture, stories, and traditions with genuine warmth.

Wildlife Conservation

Located next to Chitwan National Park, we actively support conservation efforts and educate guests about the incredible biodiversity that surrounds us.
